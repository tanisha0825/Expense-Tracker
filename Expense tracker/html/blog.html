Side Hustle Ideas

Side hustle ideas refer to potential ways individuals can earn extra income outside of their primary job or source of income. These ideas often leverage a person's skills, interests, resources, or available time to create additional earning opportunities.

Budget

A budget is a financial plan that helps you manage your income and expenses. It allows you to track where your money is coming from, where it's going, and how much you have left over.
